📜 Letters That Carried the Dream

To Roman Ormandy

A message of gratitude to the visionary behind trueSpace.

To Melinda Gates

A call to recognize trueSpace’s educational power.

To Bill Gates

Dear Bill,

I write to you not as a technician, nor as a nostalgic user, but as a father and a believer in education. trueSpace was not just software—it was a tool that allowed children to create, animate, and imagine freely. It was simple, intuitive, and powerful. It was a gift.

When Microsoft acquired Caligari and discontinued trueSpace, something beautiful was lost. I ask you, with all sincerity, to consider releasing the source code. Let it live again—not for profit, but for education. Let it be a tool for the children of children.

I am 71 years old. I have lived through every wave of computing. I have seen tools come and go. But trueSpace was different. It deserves to be remembered. It deserves to be revived.

With respect and hope,
Roberto Tracogna
Italy
